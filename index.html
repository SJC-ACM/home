"Here at the St. Joseph's ACM club we love to hang out, eat pizza, play video games, as well as get to know others within our field!"
- Jared Scarito

The ACM Club is the Computer Science club at St. Joseph's College. We have a driven focus to expand our understanding of the computing disciples while helping others learn along the way. Our club actively participates in the computing world by holding workshops, inviting guest speakers from the field to talk about their careers, as well as holding video game tournaments! Our club meetings usually take place every Wednesday at common hour. Feel free to stop by to casually chill with others in the field as well as play some PS4 "Disc Jam".
